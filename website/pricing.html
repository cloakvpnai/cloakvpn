Common questions

Is post-quantum cryptography really included on Basic?

Yes. Every tunnel — Basic or Pro — runs WireGuard with a Rosenpass-derived, post-quantum pre-shared key rotated every ~120 seconds. PQC isn't a paywalled feature; it's the product.

Can I pay anonymously?

Stripe (card/Apple Pay/Google Pay) is our default. For maximum anonymity we also accept Bitcoin and Monero via BTCPay Server, and cash by mail on request. We do not require your real name — only a valid email for the account.

What happens to my data on your servers?

Nothing persistent. Our concentrators run on RAM-only tmpfs log mounts, and the service does not store traffic logs, connection timestamps, or originating IPs. Billing is separated from usage: the server that takes your payment has no idea who you are on the network.

Refunds?

7-day no-questions-asked refund on both plans. Email support within 7 days of purchase and we'll process it via Stripe.

Can I use Cloak VPN in the US / UK / EU?

Yes. VPNs are legal in all of these jurisdictions. We comply with OFAC sanctions (no service in embargoed regions), but otherwise our service is available globally.
