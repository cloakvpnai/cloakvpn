How it works

A quantum-safe handshake on every connection.

1.
Open the app, tap CONNECT

Pick a server region. Cloak provisions a peer-specific tunnel keyed to your install. Your iPhone’s cryptographic identity never leaves the device.

2.
WireGuard establishes the data plane

ChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption over UDP. Modern, fast, audited. iOS’s kill switch ensures no packet bypasses the tunnel, even during reconnection.

3.
Rosenpass adds the post-quantum layer

Every two minutes, a fresh post-quantum pre-shared key is negotiated using Classic McEliece + NIST’s ML-KEM. Even if WireGuard’s classical encryption were broken by a future quantum computer, your traffic stays sealed.

4.
No logs leave our servers

Connection metadata is held in RAM and never written to disk. We can’t comply with what we don’t collect. Read the complete privacy policy.
